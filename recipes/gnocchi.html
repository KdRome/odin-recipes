Gnocchi

Description

Gnocchi is simple to make with just three ingredients: mashed potato, flour, and egg. This recipe is one my family has used for generations.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Flour
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Boil Potatoes
  2. Mash potato, flour, and egg
  3. Knead until dough froms a ball
  4. Roll out and cut into small portions
  5. Boil Gnocchi
